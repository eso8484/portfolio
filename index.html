01 Who I Am

About Enejo
Sunday Oche

I started where most software engineers don't — deep inside hardware. My undergraduate thesis at FUTMinna was an IoT-based Automatic Transfer Switch using TRIAC technology: real circuits, real power electronics, real stakes. That foundation shaped how I build everything that came after.

When I transitioned into AI and full-stack development, I didn't just write apps — I built systems. Self-hosted n8n pipelines orchestrating LLM agents. Model Context Protocol servers connecting AI to live tools. Production platforms with Supabase backends serving real users across Nigeria.

Today I operate across two professional lanes: freelance AI Automation Consultant — helping businesses ship intelligent systems — and Instructor at Jobberman, a Mastercard Foundation initiative empowering the next generation of African tech talent.

I build at the intersection where hardware knowledge meets AI reasoning meets real market needs — a rare vantage point that lets me see solutions others miss.
